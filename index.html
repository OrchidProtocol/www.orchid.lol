TRUST-FREE MARKETPLACES

When it comes to subscriptions, you pay providers up-front and trust to get what you paid for. With Orchid’s nanopayment ecosystem, no such trust is needed - as soon as you’re not satisfied, you can find a new provider via our stake-weighted randomized algorithm.

ORCHID ACCOUNTS

Accounts can be funded with a variety of tokens, enabling users to send nanopayments to pay for services build on the Orchid platform.

PREFERRED PROVIDER DIRECTORY
